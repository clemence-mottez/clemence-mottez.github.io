Research

My research focuses on the challenges that hinder the deployment of deep learning models in clinical practice, with particular attention to issues such as bias and fairness, interpretability and explainability, test-time adaptation to distribution shifts, and model confidence.

I work closely with clinicians to understand their priorities, needs, and concerns, ensuring that my research addresses real-world problems.
